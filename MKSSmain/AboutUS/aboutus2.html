8. Working Methods of MKSS

  • Collective leadership, not hierarchical.
  • Jan Sunwais, puppet shows, and street plays to spread awareness.
  • Non-party political — they don’t contest elections but actively shape politics.

9. Challenges Faced

  • Bureaucratic resistance and political pushback.
  • Threats to activists and whistleblowers.
  • Maintaining grassroots accountability while scaling impact.

10. Conclusion

MKSS is not just an organization—it is a people’s philosophy rooted in justice and democracy. It proves that laws like RTI were not given by the state, but won by the people through organized struggle...
